Testimonials

What People Say About Salaam Zindagi

The Festival of Sharing initiative is truly commendable. This noble cause not only provides essential winter clothing to those in need but also actively involves our youth in meaningful community service. It is inspiring to see young people channeling their energy and compassion towards making a positive impact. I applaud Salaam Zindagi for fostering this spirit of giving and responsibility among the next generation. My heartfelt appreciation goes out to all the volunteers and supporters for their dedicated efforts.

Sandeep Hans IAS

Director Higher Education. Chandigarh Adminstration. UT

I have been associated with Salaam Zindagi since its inception and have witnessed the organization's incredible transformation. The dedication and focus of its leaders on community service are truly commendable. Every year, around 8,000 to 9,000 NSS volunteers participate in the Festival of Sharing, staying connected with the community through Salaam Zindagi's efforts. It is an honor to support such a noble cause and see the positive impact it has on our youth and society

Dr. Bikram Rana

State Liaison Officer, Dept. of Education, Chandigarh UT

We have been associated with Salaam Zindagi since the 2015 Chennai floods. As representatives of the Tamil community, we are profoundly grateful for the tremendous help extended by this NGO during that challenging time. Salaam Zindagi's unwavering support made a significant difference, and since then, Chandigarh Tamil Mandram has proudly partnered with them. The dedication and compassion shown by Salaam Zindagi continue to inspire us, and we look forward to many more years of collaboration in serving the community together.

Raja Shekharan

General Secretary, Chandigarh Tamil Sangam

We are deeply thankful to Salaam Zindagi for stepping forward during the disaster, securing relief and food materials, and ensuring they reached those truly in need. From the entire committee and everyone who suffered during this calamity, I extend my heartfelt gratitude to Salaam Zindagi and all those who extended their help. Your dedication and support made a significant difference in our time of need. Thank you.

Madhu Bhatt

Badrinath Kedarnath Management Committee, Uttarakhand
